## Getting Started

### Registration
1. Click **Sign Up** in the top right corner.
2. Select your role: **Buyer**, **Seller**, or **Agent**.
3. Fill in your details or sign up with Google/MetaMask.

### Dashboard
Once logged in, you'll see your personalized dashboard:
- **Buyers:** Saved searches, favorite properties, and tour schedule.
- **Agents:** Lead board, analytics, and active listings.
- **Admins:** System overview and user management.

---

## For Buyers

### Searching for Properties
- Use the **Search Bar** on the home page.
- Filter by **Price**, **Location**, **Type**, and **Vastu Score**.
- Use the **Map View** to explore neighborhoods.

### Property Details
Each listing includes:
- High-quality images and virtual tours.
- **Vastu Compliance Score** (0-100).
- **Climate Risk Analysis**.
- Investment potential calculator.

### Scheduling Tours
1. On a property page, click **Schedule Tour**.
2. Choose **In-Person** or **Virtual**.
3. Select a date and time.
4. You will receive a confirmation email.

## For Agents

### Managing Leads
- Go to **Agent Dashboard** > **Leads**.
- Drag and drop leads between **New**, **Contacted**, **Qualified**, and **Closed**.
- Click a lead to view details and add notes.

### Analytics
- View your **Performance Analytics** tab.
- Track **Total Views**, **Inquiries**, and **Conversion Rates**.
- Analyze trends to improve your listings.

### Client Communication
- Use the **Messages** tab to chat with potential buyers.
- Send quick replies or schedule meetings directly within the chat.

---

## For Admins

### User Management
- Navigate to **Admin Panel** > **Users**.
- View all registered users.
- **Edit Roles:** Promote users to Agents or Admins.
- **Suspend/Activate:** Manage user access.

### System Health
- Monitor server status, database connections, and API latency from the **System Health** dashboard.

## Advanced Features

### Blockchain & NFTs
- **Property NFTs:** Every verified property is minted as an NFT on the Polygon network.
- **Fractional Ownership:** Invest in high-value properties by buying fractional shares (tokens).

### AI Insights
- **Vastu AI:** Upload a floor plan to get an instant Vastu analysis.
- **Price Prediction:** AI-driven estimates based on market trends.
